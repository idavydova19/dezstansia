About SafeHaven Pest Control

Founded in 2010, SafeHaven Pest Control has been providing top-notch pest management services to homes and businesses in the community for over a decade. Our mission is to create pest-free environments using safe, effective, and environmentally friendly methods.

Our Commitment

At SafeHaven, we are committed to:

  • Using the latest pest control technologies and techniques
  • Providing exceptional customer service
  • Employing highly trained and certified technicians
  • Offering eco-friendly pest control solutions
  • Educating our clients on pest prevention
